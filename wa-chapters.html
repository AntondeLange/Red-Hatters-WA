Start a New Chapter

Don't see a chapter in your area? Start your own and bring the Red Hat Society to your community!

How to Start a Chapter

  1. Gather Interest: Find at least 5 women who want to join
  2. Choose Leadership: Select a Queen and officers
  3. Register: Complete the chapter registration form
  4. Plan Activities: Organize your first meeting or event
  5. Connect: Join our network of WA chapters

Chapter Benefits

  • Support from the Red Hat Society organization
  • Access to resources and materials
  • Connection to other chapters
  • Event planning assistance
  • Member recruitment support
