Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Red Hat Society membership, activities, and how to get started.

What is the Red Hat Society?

The Red Hat Society is a global organization for women 50 and over, founded on the principles of fun, friendship, freedom, fulfillment, and fitness. We encourage women to embrace aging with joy and celebrate life through social activities, events, and sisterhood.

How do I join the Red Hat Society?

+

Joining is easy! You can become a member by:

  • Visiting our website and clicking "Join"
  • Filling out the membership form
  • Paying the annual membership fee
  • Finding a local chapter or starting your own

What are the age requirements?

Members must be 50 years of age or older. However, we also welcome "Pink Hatters" - women under 50 who can join as associate members and participate in most activities while waiting to become full Red Hat Society members.

What does it cost to join?

+

Annual membership fees vary by region, but typically range from $25-$35 per year. This includes access to our members-only website, digital magazine, and exclusive member benefits. Some local chapters may have additional small fees for activities and events.

Do I have to wear a red hat and purple outfit?

+

While the red hat and purple outfit are our signature look and encouraged at official events, they're not mandatory for every gathering. Many chapters have casual events where members can dress as they please. The important thing is to have fun and be yourself!

What activities do chapters typically do?

+

Chapter activities vary widely and are limited only by imagination! Common activities include:

  • Luncheons and dinners
  • Museum visits and cultural events
  • Travel and day trips
  • Arts and crafts workshops
  • Charity and volunteer work
  • Games and social gatherings

Can I start my own chapter?

+

Absolutely! If there isn't a chapter in your area, you can start your own. As a Queen (chapter leader), you'll receive special resources and support from Hatquarters. You'll need at least 5 members to officially charter a new chapter.

What if there's no chapter near me?

+

If there's no local chapter, you can join as an "At-Large" member and participate in online activities, virtual events, and connect with members worldwide. You can also start your own chapter or join our global online community.

What are the benefits of membership?

+

Membership benefits include:

  • Access to our members-only website
  • Digital magazine (2x yearly)
  • Exclusive member discounts
  • Global sisterhood connections
  • Chapter activities and events
  • Leadership opportunities
  • Volunteer and charity work

How often do chapters meet?

+

Meeting frequency varies by chapter, but most chapters meet monthly or bi-monthly. Some chapters have more frequent gatherings, while others meet quarterly. The schedule is determined by the chapter members and their Queen based on everyone's availability and interests.

Still Have Questions?

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, don't hesitate to reach out to us. We're here to help you get started on your Red Hat Society journey!
