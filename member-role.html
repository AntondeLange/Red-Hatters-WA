Understanding Your Role

Every member plays an important part in our community, whether you're a new member or a seasoned Queen.

Chapter Queen

Lead your chapter with grace and enthusiasm. Queens organize events, welcome new members, and ensure everyone feels included and valued in our sisterhood.

  • Organize chapter meetings and events
  • Welcome and mentor new members
  • Communicate with Hatquarters
  • Maintain chapter records

Vice Queen

Support your Queen and step up when needed. Vice Queens help with planning, member coordination, and ensuring smooth chapter operations.

  • Assist with event planning
  • Support new member integration
  • Help with chapter communications
  • Fill in when Queen is unavailable

Chapter Scribe

Keep track of our chapter's activities and memories. Scribes document our adventures and help preserve our shared experiences.

  • Record meeting minutes
  • Maintain member contact lists
  • Document chapter activities
  • Create chapter newsletters

Chapter Treasurer

Manage our chapter's finances responsibly. Treasurers handle dues, event costs, and ensure financial transparency for all members.

  • Collect and manage chapter dues
  • Track event expenses
  • Maintain financial records
  • Provide financial reports

Event Coordinator

Bring the fun to life! Event coordinators plan and execute our social gatherings, outings, and special celebrations.

  • Plan chapter events and outings
  • Coordinate with venues
  • Manage RSVPs and attendance
  • Ensure events run smoothly

Member at Large

Every member contributes to our community in their own special way. Your participation, enthusiasm, and friendship make our sisterhood stronger.

  • Attend chapter meetings and events
  • Welcome new members warmly
  • Participate in activities
  • Share ideas and suggestions
