Welcome to the Red Hat Society

Celebrating women over 50 with fun, friendship, and frivolity

Our Mission

The Red Hat Society is a global organization of women who come together to celebrate life, friendship, and the joy of being over 50. We believe in the power of sisterhood, the importance of play, and the value of embracing our age with pride and humor.

What We Do

We organize social events, travel adventures, craft workshops, dining experiences, and community service projects. Our activities are designed to bring women together in a supportive, fun, and inclusive environment where everyone can be themselves.

Why Join Us

Membership in the Red Hat Society offers lifelong friendships, exciting adventures, personal growth opportunities, and a sense of belonging to a community that celebrates women at every stage of life.

Membership Information

Everything you need to know about becoming and being a member

How to Join

  1. Complete the online registration form
  2. Pay the annual membership fee
  3. Attend a new member orientation
  4. Choose or be assigned to a chapter
  5. Receive your welcome packet and red hat!

Membership Benefits

  • Access to exclusive member events
  • Member directory and networking
  • Resource library and downloads
  • Chapter activities and socials
  • Travel opportunities and chapter trips
  • Community service projects
  • Online forums and discussions
  • Newsletter and updates

Annual Dues

Annual membership dues are $25 per year and include:

  • National Red Hat Society membership
  • Chapter membership
  • Welcome packet with red hat
  • Access to all member benefits
  • Subscription to the Red Hat Society magazine

Chapter Organization

How chapters work and leadership roles

Chapter Structure

Each chapter is led by a Queen (president) and supported by officers including Vice Queen, Scribe, and Treasurer. Chapters typically meet monthly and organize various activities throughout the year.

Leadership Roles

Chapter leadership positions are voluntary and rotate regularly. All members are encouraged to take on leadership roles at some point in their membership journey.

Chapter Activities

Chapters organize a variety of activities including social gatherings, craft workshops, dining events, cultural outings, and community service projects.

Events & Activities

Participating in and organizing Red Hat events

Event Types

  • Social Events: Luncheons, teas, parties, and gatherings
  • Cultural Events: Museum visits, theater outings, concerts
  • Travel Events: Day trips, weekend getaways, international tours
  • Craft Events: Workshops, classes, and creative activities
  • Service Events: Community service and charity work

Event Planning

Members are encouraged to suggest and help organize events. The chapter's Event Coordinator works with members to plan and execute successful activities that appeal to the chapter.

RSVP Guidelines

Please RSVP to events promptly and honor your commitments. If you must cancel, give as much notice as possible so others can be accommodated.
