Pink Hatters

The future Red Hatters - women under 50 who are part of our amazing sisterhood

Welcome to Pink Hatters

While the Red Hat Society is seen as being a group for women over 50, women under 50 have also been part of the Red Hat Society since 2000, and are called "Pink Hatters".

Women over 50 wear the red and purple colours, while women under 50 instead wear pink hats with lavender/lilac clothing. Once they reach 50 they can "REDuate" and don the red hat and purple clothing of a Red Hatter.

Pink Hatters can do everything that Red Hatters can do, they can join chapters alongside the Red Hatters and they can even be Queens of their own chapters.

Sisterhood & Support

Red Hatters often feel that Hatting is important to them because it gives them a sense of sisterhood, support and fun, and stops them from being invisible in society.

Positive Aging

For women in their 30s and 40s, Hatting can help show them that growing older isn't something negative. The fun and enjoyment of life certainly does not need to end!

Future Leaders

Reaching the age of 50 then becomes something to look forward to, rather than something to shy away from. Our Pink Hatting "Pinkies" are the Red Hatters of the future!

"Our Pink Hatting 'Pinkies' are the Red Hatters of the future!"
— Red Hat Society Philosophy

The Journey to 50

Pink Hatters enjoy all the same benefits and activities as Red Hatters, participating in chapter events, social gatherings, and community activities. They bring fresh energy and new perspectives to our chapters while learning from the wisdom and experience of their Red Hatter sisters.

Ready to "REDuate"?

When Pink Hatters reach the age of 50, they have the exciting opportunity to "REDuate" and transition to wearing the traditional red hat and purple clothing. This milestone is celebrated as a special achievement and marks their full entry into the Red Hat Society as Red Hatters.

Ready to Join as a Pink Hatter?

Whether you're under 50 and ready to start your journey as a Pink Hatter, or over 50 and looking to join as a Red Hatter, we'd love to welcome you to our sisterhood!
