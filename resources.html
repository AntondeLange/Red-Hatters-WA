Hoot Ideas

Looking for ideas for your next Red Hatting get-together? Browse our comprehensive collection of activity ideas, themes, and event suggestions to make your gatherings unforgettable!

Daily Celebrations Hoot Ideas

Mystery Dinner

Wacky dinner served at home with surprise courses and themed decorations.

Bag Lady Fashion Show

Show off your most creative and outrageous outfits in a fun fashion parade.

Literal Fashion Show

Dress up as your royal title or create literal interpretations of fashion themes.

Shopping Centre Scavenger Hunts

Organize fun scavenger hunts at local shopping centres with creative challenges.

Active Ideas

Mini Golf / Croquet / Bowling

Fun group activities that everyone can enjoy regardless of skill level.

Ice Skating / Rollerskating

Glide into fun with these classic group activities.

Visit the Snow

Plan a winter wonderland adventure for those who love the cold.

Pool Party / Beach Party / Dance Party

Water fun, sand, and dancing - perfect for any season!

Dance Lessons

Learn new moves together - from ballroom to line dancing.

Hot Air Ballooning

Soar above the landscape for a truly memorable experience.

Fishing

Relaxing day by the water with friends and fishing rods.

Horse Riding

Enjoy the countryside on horseback with guided trail rides.

Cafe/Restaurant & "Going Out" Ideas

Yum Cha / Banquet / High Tea

Elegant dining experiences perfect for special celebrations.

Ice Cream Parlour or Dessert Only Dinner

Sweet treats and indulgence - because dessert first is always a good idea!

PJ Party in a Cafe/Restaurant

Comfortable and cozy dining in your most comfortable attire.

Go to the Theatre/Cinema

Cultural outings to enjoy shows, movies, and performances together.

Casino

Try your luck with friends in a fun, responsible gaming environment.

Number Dinner

Choose a number and try to incorporate that into your dinner as much as possible. For example, if you choose number 7, you could pick a restaurant that has #7 as the street number, eating at 7pm, eating the 7th item on the menu or selecting items that cost $7 or have a 7 in the price etc.

Progressive Dinners

Start your meal off at one venue, then move to a new venue for the next course.

Valentines Dinner

Bring husbands along for a romantic group dinner celebration.

General Ideas

Dress as Your Royal Title

For a bit of fun have everyone dress up as something to do with their Royal title! So for example Countess Champagne might decorate her dress with champagne corks.

Calendar

Create a calendar at the end of the year from photos of your Chapter from throughout the year. You could even do a funny "sexy" calendar with each member taking on a month and having a glamorous photo shoot.

Debutante Ball

For the ladies who haven't had one, have a Red Hatter one!

Mystery Car Trip

Carpool and follow in convoy to a mystery location (The leader knows where to go!)

"Fake Wedding"

Where you hold a wedding ceremony for fun - complete with bridesmaids and groomsmen!

Game Show

Go on a gameshow like Family Feud or create your own version.

Celebrate "0" Birthdays

(60/70 etc.) and have a "Golduation" when turning 80.

5 Year Celebration

School uniform dressup event (kids go to school at 5).

Bus Trip

Hire a bus and go on an outing together to explore new places.

Fundraiser for Charity

Organize events to raise money for causes close to your hearts.

Masquerade Ball

Elegant masked ball with mystery and intrigue.

Gardens, Park & Outdoor Ideas

Visit Botanical Gardens / Zoo

Explore nature and wildlife in beautiful garden settings.

Picnic in a Park

Classic outdoor gathering with food, games, and fresh air.

Boat Ride

Scenic water adventures - from river cruises to sailing.

Peddle Boats on a Lake

Fun and active way to enjoy the water together.

Go to the Horse Races

Dress up and enjoy the excitement of race day.

Shopping Ideas

Go on a Shopping Spree Tour

Visit discount outlets like DFO for great bargains and fun shopping.

Antique Shopping

Explore antique stores and markets for unique finds and treasures.

Fashion Show Shopping

Shop for outfits and then have a fashion show to show off your purchases.
